Location Features

The location features help skateboarders find and discover
new skateboarding loctions!

Create and Edit Spots

You have the ability to save and create your own skate location spots

Filter Locations

The filter bar allows you to choose from skateparks, street skating or your own favourite spots!

Directions

Directions are provided with every skate location

Social Features

The social features allow skateboarders a connective
platform to discover new friends

Skate Feed

The skate feed allows you to check out skateboard images from around the world!

Camera

The camera allows you to post your best tricks for other skaters to see.

Profile

Create your own profile and follow other users to build up a a solid skating crew.

Top Posts

Search through the top posts section to discover the best skaters.

Download Spots

Visit other user profiles to download their spots and expand your skate locations!

Search

Use the search function to find skaters you alredy know.
